About Me

Salutations! Welcome to my website, my name is Juice and I am a full stack freelance web developer and freelance fingerpick style steel string acoustic guitarist. My main focus is backend Node.js and I absolutely love working in Node.js. I am still learning Node.js however my best ability is my ability to quickly learn and acquire new skills. I am also familiar with Python, LUA and some C. My hobbies are coding, video games and music! I often mix my hobbies together and I love to code games. I am currently working on a music game involving the Raspberry Pi. I have once worked in IT and am very familiar with computer hardware. I feel very comfortable working with hardware because I once worked at an escape room learning the Arduino Uno to make puzzles. On the weekends, I am a guitar teacher and I teach a group class for beginners to intermediate guitar. My most favorite temporary job was being a debugger by being a game tester, the skills I learned as a debugger helped me tremendously in coding. Some other skills involve being an editor, community manager, moderator for Twitch streamers.
